# HabitFlow — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 18, 2026_

HabitFlow ("the app", "we") is a habit-tracking app published by the HabitFlow
developer. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. We designed
HabitFlow to keep your data on your device.

## Summary

- **No account required.** There is no sign-up, login, or HabitFlow server.
- **Your habit data stays on your device.** Habits, completions, streaks, and
  coach chat history are stored locally in an on-device database (SQLite).
- **We do not sell your data** and we do not run third-party advertising or
  analytics SDKs.

## What data the app stores on your device

- **Habits and progress:** names, icons, schedules, reminder times, completion
  history, and streaks.
- **AI coach chat history:** messages you exchange with the in-app coach (Aria).
- **Settings:** your notification preference and, if you provide one, your
  Anthropic API key. The API key is stored locally on your device only.

This data never leaves your device except as described below.

## Third-party AI processing (optional)

The AI coach features are **optional** and only work if you add your own
Anthropic API key in Settings. When you use the AI coach or the daily insight:

- A summary of your habit data (habit names, streaks, completion rates, and the
  messages you send) is transmitted to **Anthropic** (api.anthropic.com) using
  your own API key, solely to generate the coach's response.
- This processing is governed by Anthropic's privacy policy:
  https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
- If you do not add an API key, no data is sent to Anthropic; the app uses
  on-device offline messages instead.

## Notifications

With your permission, HabitFlow schedules **local** notifications on your device
to remind you about habits. These reminders are generated on-device and are not
sent through any external push service.

## Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by **Google Play Billing**. We do not
receive or store your payment details; Google handles all payment information.

## Data deletion

You can delete any habit (and its history) inside the app at any time.
Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data, including your API key and
chat history.

## Children's privacy

HabitFlow is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children
under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected
by the "Last updated" date above.
